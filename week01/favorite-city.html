My Favorite City

Libreville is my favorite city and it's also my capital. The city hosts around 900 000 inhabitants and is very beautiful. We Gabonese live in Libreville and apreciate the peaceful aspect of the city. Libreville has different spots were you can relax chill and also have fun. One of them is "La baie des rois" a really nice spot were you can relax and have a good time with friends and family.
